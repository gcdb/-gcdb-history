Event World Raid Puringpooh Information

■ [Drop Event] Puringpooh Invitation

■ Drop Event Duration
20 Feb 2020 (Thursday) After maintenance ~ 4 March 2020 (Wednesday) 23:59

■ Event Details
During the event, clearing [Boss Battle], [SP Dungeon] and [Raid] will drop [Puringpooh Invitation]. You will be able to sell the [Puringpooh Invitation] from 5 March 00:00 onwards.

■ [Puringpooh Invitation] Obtainable Info

※ You will be able to take part in the [Event World Raid] by using [Puringpooh Invitation].

■ [Event World Raid] Puringpooh

・ Upon clearing Chapter 4-41 you will be able to unlock the event world raid.

■ Event Duration
20 Feb 2020 (Thursday) After maintenance ~ 4 March 2020 (Wednesday) 23:59

※ You will be able to take part in the [Event World Raid] for a max of 5 times a day.

※ The daily limit resets at 00:00 daily.

■ Event Details
Battle together with 2 ~ max 4 players real time to defeat Puringpooh.
Exchange [Puringpooh Medal dropped by defeating Puringpooh at the exchange shop for various rewards.

※ If you retire from the raid or your communication to the game server is cut off, one of your 5 daily attempts will be consumed and you will not be able to re-enter the raid that's in progress. Compensation will not be given so please be sure to have a stable connection.

■ How to participate
・ During the event, use [Puringpooh Invitation] dropped from [Boss Battle], [SP Dungeon], [Raid] to enter with a max of 3 other players to defeat Puringpooh.

※ To enter the raid, 30 [Puringpooh Invitation] of the party leader as well as daily entrance count will be consumed. Party members will only have their daily entrance count consumed.
※ Regardless of the difficulties, be it [Normal], [Hard] or [Extreme], they will all cost 1 entrance.
The more people participating in the raid, the more rewards you will get. (The max amount of reward will be obtained with 4 players.)
※ Rewards that can be earned are distributed taking the following into consideration: party leader, ranking, size of party and the amount of damage dealt.
The enemy's battle pattern will be changed depending on the damage dealt to him 3 turns after the raid starts.

■ [Event World Raid] battle Ssytem

① Your total damage dealt to Puringpooh
※ Tap to see more details

② Damage ranking in battle
※ Your rep character will be displayed, tap on the player icon to see their damage

③ Enemy's battle pattern
※ The battle gauge will be filled up in various ways. When the gauge is full, Puringpooh's battle battern will change.

■ [Puringpooh Medal] Exchange Shop

You will be able to exchange [Puringpooh Medal] for various rewards such as [Part 2 SSR Ticket], [Costume Upgrade Material], [SSR Pendant] etc. in the exchange shop.

■ Exchange Shop Duration
20 Feb 2020 (Thursday) After maintenance ~ 8 March 2020 (Sunday) 23:59
