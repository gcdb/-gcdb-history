Recommended Usage
Simon has a very good kit for a damage dealer, but he is held back by his R stats. His ultimate can hit very hard if ran alongside blue Lilia, who can give him additional Pierce Rate. In PvP, Simon can be a decent sub slot option as he boosts the overall damage of all allies by 15%.
Recommended Gears
For sub slot: HP/DEF
Substats: ATK, DEF, HP
Since his main use would be a PvP sub unit, you want HP/DEF on him to compensate for the CP missing out due to his R stats.
